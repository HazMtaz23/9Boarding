üìê Deal Update Structure

Study the standard formats before drafting. Use these templates to guide concise, consistent output.

Structure Reference

Single tranche format

Amount Security due YYYY. PT Margin @ Price, Floor. Timing sentence.

Example:

$500m TLB due 2033. PT S+300 @ 100, 0% floor. Commits due 30-Sep @ 12:00 ET

Dual-tranche format

Amount dual-tranche TLBs due YYYY. Tranches & Timing:

Body sections:

  • Tranches: Each tranche on its own line with PT
  • Additional UoP / Stepdowns / Ticking fees (optional line(s))
  • Timing: Call & commitments deadlines

Example:

$500m dual-tranche TLBs due 2033. Tranches & Timing:

Tranches:

$250m TLB due 2033. PT S+300 @ 100, 0% floor

$250m TLB due 2033. PT S+300 @ 100, 0% floor

Additional UoP: LBO

Timing:

Lender call today (30-Sep) @ 12:00 ET

Commits due tomorrow (1-Oct) @ 12:00 ET

Tips

  • Keep opening line ultra concise ‚Äì no issuer name if already clear from context.
  • Use consistent ordering: Amount ‚Üí Instrument ‚Üí Maturity ‚Üí Pricing ‚Üí Timing.
  • Floor only if non-zero (still show 0% floor for training consistency here).
  • Separate multi-tranche detail under the Tranches header; avoid run-on sentences.
