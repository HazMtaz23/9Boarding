Scenario 1 — Ratings alias miss

A ratings change isn't linked to the issuer due to alias mismatch and lands in Changes. What steps do you take?

Show model answer

Fix issuer alias mapping; re-trigger linking; publish with correct rating action tags if material; otherwise discard as duplicate.

Scenario 2 — Duplicate crawler update

A firm-site crawler posts a duplicate update. Decide whether to ignore or publish and why.

Show model answer

Discard exact duplicates; publish only if the new item adds material information (e.g., corrected figures) and ensure entity links are intact.
